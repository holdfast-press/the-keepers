A six-book contemporary fantasy series by R.J. Thornton, published by
Holdfast Press. About a hidden council of memory-bearers who discover,
slowly and impossibly, that the bindings their long-dead bloodlines
once held are failing one by one.

This repository holds the working files for the series — manuscripts,
outlines, character bibles, branding assets, and marketing materials.

*What we choose to carry, carries us.*
